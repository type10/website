<a href="https://vitao.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Vitao</a> is our own product: an AI-powered résumé builder that helps job seekers create ATS-optimized résumés — with an honest one-time price instead of a subscription trap.

## Why we built it

We don't just advise on product engineering; we live it. Vitao is where we prove our [Product Engineering](/services/product-engineering/) practice on our own dime — shipping, operating and improving a real SaaS product with real users.

## What's inside

- ATS-optimized templates engineered to pass recruiter screening systems
- AI assistance for summaries, bullet points and résumé review
- Job-matching analysis with tailoring suggestions and gap scoring
- PDF and Word export, privacy-first, one fair one-time price

Operating Vitao keeps us honest: we live with the same release pressure, AI trade-offs and reliability work we ask clients to trust us with. <a href="https://vitao.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Try Vitao</a> for yourself.
