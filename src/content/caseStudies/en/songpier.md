Songpier (Pierlane) was a music platform that turned songs into shareable, app-like experiences. TYPE10 Media helped build its creator tooling.

## What we did

We conceived and built the **Songpier Studio** (Backbone.js, RequireJS, SASS/Compass) — the web application creators used to assemble their content.

We also supported the Songpier team in migrating the **Songpier API to Symfony2 & Doctrine**, modernising the platform underneath the studio.

## Why it mattered

A creator tool is only as good as it feels to use. Pairing a focused single-page studio with a cleaner API gave the team a base they could iterate on quickly.
