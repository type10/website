organize.me was a personal document-management and organisation service. TYPE10 Media joined to extend its platform.

## What we did

We developed features and new components across a set of **Restify (Node.js) based micro services**.

Along the way, we landed a few **open-source contributions to the swagger-ui project** on GitHub — fixes that helped us and everyone else documenting APIs the same way.

## Why it mattered

Good micro-service work is rarely glamorous: it's clear contracts, documented APIs and components the next developer can pick up without a meeting. That's the kind of work that keeps a platform healthy.
