RTL+ (previously TVNow) is one of Germany's major streaming services. TYPE10 Media joined to consult on and extend its Smart-TV experience.

## What we did

We consulted on and **developed new features for the RTL+ Smart-TV OTT app**, working across a **React** frontend and **Node.js / NestJS** services on the backend.

## Why it mattered

OTT apps on living-room hardware are an unforgiving environment: constrained devices, big audiences and no room for a janky experience. Solid frontend engineering and well-shaped services are what keep the picture smooth.
