<a href="https://kammer-revision.de" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kammer-Revision</a> is a civic-tech platform we built for Freiheitskanzlei. More than 150 chambers in Germany — from trade and the IHK to the medical, legal and tax-advisor chambers — charge mandatory membership fees. Kammer-Revision makes measurable what they actually deliver in return: *"Wir prüfen die Prüfer"* — we audit the auditors.

## What we did

TYPE10 Media owned this one end to end. We shaped the **technical concept** and **shipped the entire platform** — from architecture to launch.

- A guided, step-by-step legal workflow: information request → objection → an eight-dimension scoring matrix, producing ready-to-send documents instead of legalese.
- A public **transparency index** that turns every response into an anonymized data point, building the first objective comparison of Germany's chamber landscape.
- A contribution calculator and quick-check so members can see, in minutes, what their mandatory membership costs and whether it's worth a closer look.

## Why it mattered

A mandatory fee only justifies itself through performance. By making each chamber's work comparable, Kammer-Revision turns a one-sided obligation into accountability — and it slots into the Freiheitskanzlei family of citizen tools as a self-contained, [product-engineered](/services/product-engineering/) platform we delivered from concept to production.
