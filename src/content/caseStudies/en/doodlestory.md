DoodleStory is TYPE10 Media's own product — a hilarious drawing game where doodles and short descriptions are passed around a group and become epic, surprising stories.

## What we did

We designed and built the whole thing. The game was released in **2016 on the Apple App Store and Google Play** and developed in **React & Flux**.

The backend is a **Node.js REST API** that stores its data in **Elasticsearch** and is deployed on the **AWS cloud**.

## Why it mattered

Building and shipping our own product keeps us honest. DoodleStory is part of a long habit of betting on our own engineering — the same habit that produced [Vitao](https://vitao.io).
