CHECK24 is Germany's largest comparison platform. TYPE10 Media built the first iteration of its **private health insurance (PKV) comparison**.

## What we did

The backend gathered its data from third-party insurers, with a great deal of effort put into **caching that data efficiently in Redis** so the comparison stayed fast even under load.

On the frontend, we used **React**, where usability was everything: the whole experience was built around guiding the user through a comparison form that could otherwise feel overwhelming.

## Why it mattered

Insurance comparison lives and dies by trust and clarity. A fast, well-guided form turns a daunting decision into something a visitor can actually complete.
