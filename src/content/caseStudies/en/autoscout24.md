AutoScout24 is one of Europe's largest online automotive marketplaces, connecting millions of buyers and sellers across the continent.

## What we did

TYPE10 Media joined the effort to break a single-AWS-account architecture apart, helping extract and migrate services into their own dedicated AWS accounts and analysing where the new structure could unlock cost savings.

A big win we helped land was migrating a business-critical service from standalone EC2 instances to a managed [spot fleet](/services/cloud-devops/) — keeping it resilient while running at a fraction of the on-demand cost.

## Why it mattered

A single shared account blurs ownership, cost attribution and blast radius: one team's mistake can ripple across everything. Separate accounts give each service clear boundaries, isolated billing and room to optimise independently — and the spot-fleet migration showed just how much cost was hiding in always-on, on-demand capacity.
