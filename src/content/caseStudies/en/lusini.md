Lusini is a B2B marketplace for hospitality and catering supplies. TYPE10 Media joined its scrum team to help build the next generation of the store.

## What we did

We supported the team on two fronts:

- the **store frontend** (Backbone.js, RequireJS, Handlebars, Stylus, Bootstrap), and
- a new **Node.js API backend** (Rendr, Express, Elasticsearch),

all delivered in a test-driven **continuous-integration** process (Jenkins, Mocha, Chai, Karma).

## Why it mattered

Working inside the client's scrum team — with tests and CI from day one — meant features shipped steadily and the codebase stayed something the in-house team could keep building on.
