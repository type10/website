We bring the same engineering discipline to Web3 that we bring to the rest of the stack — pragmatic, tested and production-grade, without the hype.

## What we do

- **Smart contracts** — EVM/Solidity development with a test-first mindset and an eye on audits.
- **Decentralized apps (dApps)** — wallet-connected frontends and the integrations behind them (ethers.js, web3.js).
- **Protocol integrations** — Chainlink oracles, The Graph indexing and on-chain data pipelines.
- **Architecture reviews** — honest assessments of whether a problem actually needs a blockchain at all.

## How we work

We treat smart-contract code as what it is: software that moves value and can't be quietly patched. That means tests, reviews and clear documentation before anything touches mainnet.
