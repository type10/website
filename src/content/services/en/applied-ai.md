Most "AI features" are a chatbot in the corner that nobody uses. We build the other kind: AI that does real work inside your product, measured against outcomes — and we prove it on our own product before we bill you for it.

## What we do

- **AI assistants & copilots** — LLM features woven into the product where they genuinely help, with the prompts, guardrails and fallbacks that keep them dependable in front of real users.
- **Autonomous agents** — agentic systems that plan, call tools and complete multi-step work, with a human in the loop wherever the stakes demand one.
- **Retrieval & context** — RAG pipelines, embeddings and vector search that ground models in your data instead of letting them guess.
- **Evaluation & operations** — evals, monitoring and cost controls, so quality is measured rather than assumed and the bill never surprises you.

<a href="https://vitao.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Vitao</a>, our résumé builder, leans heavily on AI to coach users toward a stronger CV — so we ship and operate this work on our own dime, not just for clients.
