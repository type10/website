We help engineering teams move to the cloud — and stay calm once they're there. From the first architecture diagram to the pipeline that ships on a Friday afternoon, we plan, build and automate the platform your product runs on.

## What we do

- **Cloud architecture & migration** — AWS-first design, re-platforming and pragmatic lift-and-shift, with honest cost and reliability reviews.
- **Kubernetes & containers** — cluster design, Helm, autoscaling and zero-downtime rollouts.
- **CI/CD automation** — build, test and deploy pipelines that give every commit a clear, repeatable path to production.
- **Observability** — metrics, logs and tracing so you see problems before your users do.

## How we work

We embed with your team and leave it stronger: documented infrastructure, runbooks and pipelines your own engineers operate — never a black box only we understand. When we helped split [Maxdome's](/work/maxdome/) monolith into micro services, the goal was always a platform the in-house team could run themselves.
